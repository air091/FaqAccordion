FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and paid challenges. The free tier gives you access to a selection of projects to start with.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can use the projects you build on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio to showcase your skills to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can get help by asking the community, reading through the solution reports, or referring to the resources provided in the challenge.
